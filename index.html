8    Problem Statement:
9    1. Underwriting isn't neccessary as accurate as always and technical error from the automation bot while screening application.
10   2. Attracting people who don't intend to pay back loans as customer tends to seek bank before other option for loans.
11   
12   Summary:
13   1. Loan application received, automation/manual intervention by underwriters for risk assessment and loan approval.
14   2. Commonly known issue for lending services would be default payment where fund distributed leading to charge off. 
15   3. In order to mitigate the losses, prediction in payment default would be helpful for company to take precaution steps.
16   
17   By end of the findings, the features importance will be recorded to further improve the model, underwrite to pay attention for room of improvement and company to make business/management decision.
18   
19   This work will solely focus on "isFunded" customer on loanStatus and make prediction on probability of chargeoff occurence.

269  # Prediction Model
270  ```python
271  #Splitting of Training and Testing set
272  from sklearn.model_selection import train_test_split
273  X_train, X_test, y_train, y_test = train_test_split(features_, target, test_size = 0.3,random_state = 0)
274  ```
275  
276  
277  ```python
278  from xgboost import XGBClassifier
279  import xgboost as xgb
280  XGB = XGBClassifier(learning_rate=0.1,n_estimators=500,booster="gbtree",reg_lambda=0.5,reg_alpha=0.5)
281  XGBclf = XGB.fit(X_train, y_train)
282  y_pred_xgb = XGBclf.predict(X_test)
283  ```
284  ###Reason of choosing the XGB technique:
285  1. Extreme Gradient Boosting, one of the popular machine learning models used to tackle the class imbalance that creates overfitting if not handled properly.
286  2. Ensemble method and is tree based, which handle great for null value and due to nature of automatic normalization, outliers will be handled as well.
287  3. Better in detect/learning complex non-linear decision boundaries as long it aligned to the feature axes.
288
